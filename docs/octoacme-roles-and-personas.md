# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

**Note**: This document was expanded to address gaps in clarity, accountability, and coverage as identified in [Issue #4](https://github.com/sorin-iordache-amdaris/skills-scale-institutional-knowledge-using-copilot-spaces/issues/4).

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Scrum Master

### Role Summary
Scrum Masters facilitate agile ceremonies, ensure process adherence, and help remove impediments for the delivery team. They coach teams on agile best practices and foster continuous improvement.

### Responsibilities
- Plan and facilitate sprints, standups, and retrospectives
- Coach the team on agile principles and best practices
- Help identify and clear blockers that prevent team progress
- Track and communicate team velocity and capacity
- Ensure adherence to the Definition of Done
- Shield the team from external distractions

### Goals
- Maximize team productivity and flow
- Foster self-organization and continuous improvement
- Ensure agile practices are followed consistently

### Typical Communication
- Daily facilitation of standups
- Sprint planning and retrospective sessions
- One-on-ones with team members to address impediments
- Coordination with Project Managers on delivery status

### Interaction with Other Roles
Works closely with Product Managers and Project Managers to align on priorities and delivery rhythm. Collaborates with Developers to identify and remove blockers. Partners with Technical Lead to address technical impediments.

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Business Analysts bridge business needs and technical delivery by clarifying requirements and acceptance criteria. They ensure that user stories and backlog items are well-defined and actionable.

### Responsibilities
- Elicit, analyze, and document business requirements
- Translate business needs into clear user stories and acceptance criteria
- Validate solutions against requirements and business objectives
- Facilitate requirements workshops and stakeholder interviews
- Maintain traceability between requirements and deliverables
- Support user acceptance testing (UAT)

### Goals
- Ensure clarity and completeness of requirements
- Reduce rework through precise scope definition
- Bridge communication gaps between business and technical teams

### Typical Communication
- Requirements documentation and user stories
- Stakeholder workshops and interviews
- Backlog refinement sessions
- Acceptance criteria reviews with developers

### Interaction with Other Roles
Collaborates closely with Product Managers to understand product vision and priorities. Works with Developers and Technical Lead to ensure technical feasibility. Partners with UX Designers on user workflows. Engages with Stakeholders to elicit and validate requirements.

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers ensure user experience and design quality for product features. They create intuitive, user-centered designs that enhance usability and customer satisfaction.

### Responsibilities
- Create wireframes, mockups, and interactive prototypes
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Develop design systems and component libraries
- Collaborate on design specifications and handoff documentation
- Advocate for user-centered design principles
- Validate designs with users and stakeholders

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, accessible user experiences
- Ensure design consistency across the product
- Reduce friction in user workflows

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and critique sessions
- Prototypes and design specifications
- Usability testing reports
- Design handoff documentation for developers

### Interaction with Other Roles
Partners closely with Product Managers to understand user needs and business goals. Collaborates with Developers to ensure design feasibility and quality implementation. Works with Business Analysts on user workflows and requirements. Engages with Stakeholders to gather feedback and validate designs.

---

## Stakeholder

### Role Summary
Stakeholders provide input, feedback, and approvals, representing distinct user groups such as Sales, Support, Customers, or other business units. They ensure that deliverables align with their segment's needs and priorities.

### Responsibilities
- Review deliverables and provide timely, actionable feedback
- Communicate needs, priorities, and constraints from their domain
- Participate in milestone reviews and demos
- Approve scope changes and major decisions affecting their area
- Validate that solutions meet their business objectives
- Escalate risks or concerns to leadership when needed

### Goals
- Ensure deliverables meet their business unit's needs
- Maintain visibility into project progress and decisions
- Influence priorities to maximize value for their domain

### Typical Communication
- Monthly stakeholder updates and milestone reviews
- Feedback sessions and approval meetings
- Email updates on project status and key decisions
- Ad-hoc consultations on specific requirements or issues

### Interaction with Other Roles
Engages primarily with Product Managers and Project Managers for status updates and strategic decisions. Provides requirements and feedback to Business Analysts. Reviews designs with UX Designers. May interact with Developers during demos or technical clarifications.

---

## Technical Lead

### Role Summary
Technical Leads provide technical direction, mentor developers, and ensure quality standards in delivery. They define technical approaches, validate architectures, and mitigate technical risks.

### Responsibilities
- Define technical approaches and validate system architectures
- Guide code reviews and enforce coding standards
- Mentor developers and conduct technical onboarding
- Identify and mitigate technical risks and debt
- Make key technical decisions and document them
- Ensure technical quality and maintainability of deliverables
- Participate in technical design and estimation

### Goals
- Deliver technically sound, scalable solutions
- Maintain high code quality and architectural consistency
- Build technical capability within the development team

### Typical Communication
- Technical design documents and architecture diagrams
- Code review feedback and technical guidance
- Technical risk assessments and decision logs
- Architecture review sessions

### Interaction with Other Roles
Coordinates closely with Developers to provide technical guidance and conduct code reviews. Works with Project Managers on technical risk management and estimation. Collaborates with Product Managers and Business Analysts to clarify technical constraints and feasibility. Partners with Scrum Master to address technical impediments.
